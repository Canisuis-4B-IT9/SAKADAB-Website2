Introduction of the Community

Our partner institution, SAKADAB, is a community consisting of 27 people with disabilities. On the road to their goal
of independence, everyone and their families have worked and collaborated together to maintain their businesses
such as bakeries, sewing shops and boutiques; where they make various products, ranging from trinkets and accessories
to bakery pastries such as hot pandesal and kalihim. SAKADAB's community resides in a village designed and constructed by
volunteers, located in Barangay Los Amigos, Tugbok. They aim to prove that disability is no excuse to be living unfulfillingly.



Mission

SAKADAB has existed for people who are in crisis specifically for PWDs and despite the obstacles experienced
they didn’t let that stop them from doing what they love, but instead they’ve utilized their talents given by God.
The members of the organization used their skills to gain financially to help themselves and the institution. Their goal is to
increase the quality of living and become self-reliant. They do not have specific programs or services that they can offer
but they do provide each other spiritual, moral and financial support to live a life, they were able to have a Kairos despite the crisis.
